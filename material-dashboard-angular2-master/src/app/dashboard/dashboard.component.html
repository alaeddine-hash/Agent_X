Foundedly Performance: Magic Quadrant

Quadrant Chart from CSV Data (Chart.js)

Chart Explanation:

  • This quadrant chart splits companies by Founded Year (x-axis) and Final Score (y-axis).
  • The vertical line at 2020 separates earlier vs. more recent foundings.
  • The horizontal line at 50% of max score helps compare lower vs. higher scoring companies.
  • Colored regions indicate different performance/founding quadrants: Dinosaurs, Gorillas, Monkeys, etc.

The x-axis uses 5-year increments from 1995 to 2020, then 1-year increments from 2020 to 2026.
